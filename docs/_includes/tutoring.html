Tutoring

OPUS offers free tutoring on first year physics classes for both fall and winter terms. At least one tutor will be available during the scheduled sessions, including the main tutor for that session. Help is offered on a first come, first served basis. The schedule is updated as frequently as possible.

Tutor Availability @ 114 Allen:

*Tutors on Tuesday and Thursday slots reserve the right to end their time slot after 1 hour of tutoring.


Cram Sessions

Prior to midterms and finals, OPUS members offer a four hour session answering curated questions from previous exams and textbooks (including WileyPlus questions) to prepare you for your exams. For $20 you get in-depth explanations and techniques to solving common exam questions and the theory involved. All proceeds go towards OPUS events and bursaries.
